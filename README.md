# FMCG Sales & Marketing Analysis (2023–2025)

## Overview
End-to-end Exploratory Data Analysis on 18,240 FMCG transactions
across regions, brands, and sales channels to derive actionable
business insights.

## Tools Used
Python | Pandas | Matplotlib

## Dataset
FMCG Sales Marketing and Profitability 2023–2025 — Kaggle

## Key Findings
- Europe leads with $1.13M total profit and 217% marketing ROI
- South America has highest ROI (229%) but is underinvested
- No Promo strategy outperforms all discount campaigns by 3x
- Wholesale channel drives 36% of total revenue
- Household & Personal Care are top 2 profitable categories

## Business Recommendations
1. Increase marketing budget in Europe and South America
2. Reduce heavy discounting — No Promo yields better margins
3. Prioritize Household & Personal Care product lines
4. Invest in Online channel — currently only 21% of revenue
